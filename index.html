Simple by design

How It Works

From posting a task to getting it done — three steps, start to finish.

1

Post or Browse

Neighbors post a task with details and a fair price. Workers browse nearby tasks that fit their schedule.

2

Match & Confirm

A worker accepts the task, both sides confirm the details, and it's officially on the calendar.

3

Complete & Pay

The task gets done, payment is released securely through the app, and both sides leave a review.

Safety first

Trust & Safety

Every task connects real neighbors. We built Tasks4Teens to keep it that way.

Verified Identities

Every neighbor and worker verifies their identity before joining the platform.

Secure Payments

All payments are processed securely in-app — no cash, no card numbers exchanged.

Parental Consent

Workers under 18 require verified parent or guardian consent to start working.

Community Reviews

Ratings and reviews after every task keep the whole community accountable.
